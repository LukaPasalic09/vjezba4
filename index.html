Recipe for the best Burek

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 500g phyllo dough
  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 100ml oil

For the filling:

  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté the chopped onion until soft and translucent.
  2. Add the ground beef to the pan and cook until browned, breaking it apart as it cooks.
  3. Season the beef mixture with salt and pepper according to your taste.
  4. Preheat the oven to 200°C (392°F).
  5. Grease a baking pan with oil.
  6. Layer half of the phyllo dough sheets in the pan, brushing each layer with oil.
  7. Spread the cooked beef mixture evenly over the dough layers.
  8. Layer the remaining phyllo sheets on top, again brushing each layer with oil.
  9. Before baking, cut the burek into desired portions (squares or triangles).
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and crispy.
  11. Once baked, remove the burek from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving.

Serving:

Serve the burek warm, either as a main dish or a savory snack. It pairs well with a side of yogurt or a light salad. Burek can also be enjoyed with a cup of tea or a refreshing glass of ayran (yogurt drink).
